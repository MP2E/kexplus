Sector Specifications
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
Doom64's sectors are completly different than that of Doom, Zdoom/Hexen's format. This
Article will roughly go over the new format and all of its flags and specials.

* ---------Sector Format---------

    * short floorheight;
    * short ceilingheight;
    * unsigned short floorpic;
    * unsigned short ceilingpic;
    * unsigned short colors[5];
    * short special;
    * short tag;
    * word flags;


* ---------Sector Flags---------

    * Sound Echo filter
    * Unknown 0x2
    * Water Effect
    * Sync Specials - All sectors with this flag will sync all specials (example: multiple sectors
                      with the light flicker special will all flicker at the same time. For another
                      example, look at the staircase with the flickering light at the beginning
                      of Tech Center).
    * Unknown 0x16
    * Secret
    * Damage x5
    * Damage x10
    * Damage x20
    * Hide Subsectors In Automap (textured mode)
    * Enable Ceiling Scroll
    * Enable Floor Scroll
    * Scroll West
    * Scroll East
    * Scroll North
    * Scroll South


* ---------Sector Specials---------

    * 1: Flickering Lights
    * 2: Strobe Fast
    * 3: Strobe Slow
    * 4: Strobe Fast??
    * 8: Glowing Light
    * 9: Slow Glowing Light
    * 11: Random Glowing Light
    * 12: Sync Strobe Slow
    * 13: Sync Strobe Fast
    * 17: Fire Flicker
    * 208: Super Fast Strobe
    * 205: Start Sequence Lighting
    * 666: Demon Disposer - Sector with this flagged triggered by a linedef with special 141 will
                            instantly kill everything that is inside it.
    * 204 - 207: Fake special used to redirect a sequenced light back to the beginning.
